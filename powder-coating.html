Why Powder Coating is the Best Choice

Superior Durability

Powder coating forms a thick, uniform layer that resists chipping, scratching, fading, and corrosion far better than liquid paint. Ideal for outdoor and harsh-environment applications.

Eco-Friendly Process

No solvents or VOCs — powder coating is environmentally safe with minimal waste. Overspray is collected and reused, making it a sustainable finishing option.

Aesthetic Versatility

Achieve any color, texture, or finish — from smooth gloss to wrinkle or hammertone effects. Consistent appearance across batches for professional product presentation.

Cost-Effective

Single-coat application with no primer needed in most cases. Fast curing means quick turnaround, reducing overall project costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What colors are available for powder coating?

We can match any RAL, Pantone, or custom color sample. Most common colors (black, white, grey, beige) are in stock. Special colors may require minimum order quantities.

Can you coat parts that were fabricated elsewhere?

Yes! We accept job-work powder coating for parts fabricated anywhere. Just ensure parts are welded, ground, and free of oils or contaminants before sending them to our Vasai facility.

How long does powder coating last?

Properly applied powder coating lasts 10–20 years in typical indoor environments. Outdoor applications see 5–10 years depending on UV exposure and climate. Far superior to liquid paint.

Do you offer texture or special effect finishes?

Yes, we provide textured finishes (wrinkle, hammertone, orange peel), metallic finishes, and anti-graffiti coatings. Discuss your aesthetic requirements during quotation.

What's the lead time for powder coating?

Standard colors: 3–5 working days. Custom colors or large batches: 5–7 days. Being based in Vasai Palghar, we offer same-day pickup options for urgent jobs in Mumbai-Thane region.
