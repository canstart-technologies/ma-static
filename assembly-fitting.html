Piano hinges, butt hinges, concealed hinges, compression latches, quarter-turn locks — all types fitted with precision alignment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you source and fit hardware that I specify?

Absolutely! Provide your Bill of Materials with part numbers or specifications, and we'll source the exact hardware you need. We work with all major brands like Southco, EMKA, Schneider, and more.

Do you offer sub-assembly services?

Yes, we can handle partial assembly (e.g., only hinges and gaskets) or complete turnkey assembly including internal wiring harnesses, terminal blocks, and labeling — whatever your production needs.

Can you assemble enclosures that were fabricated elsewhere?

Yes, we accept job-work assembly for enclosures made by other fabricators. Send us the enclosures and BOM, and we'll handle the hardware fitting and quality checks at our Vasai facility.

How do you ensure proper IP rating after assembly?

We use calibrated gaskets with proper compression, torque-controlled fasteners, and sealing around all entries. Each enclosure is inspected for gasket alignment and door sealing before packing.

What's the lead time for assembly services?

If enclosures are already fabricated and coated, assembly typically takes 2–3 days for standard batches. For complete turnkey (fabrication + assembly), expect 2–3 weeks depending on complexity.
