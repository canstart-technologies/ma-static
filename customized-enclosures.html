Key Features & Customization Options

  • Custom dimensions to fit your equipment perfectly
  • Material choice: MS, SS304/316, Aluminium (0.8 mm – 6 mm)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you manufacture enclosures to custom dimensions?

Absolutely! We specialize in fully customized enclosures. Provide us with your equipment dimensions, mounting requirements, and environmental specs, and we'll design an enclosure to fit perfectly.

What IP ratings can you achieve?

We regularly manufacture enclosures rated IP54, IP55, and IP65 using proper gaskets, sealing, and cable gland entries. Higher ratings available on request with appropriate testing.

Do you provide design services if I only have a concept?

Yes! Our engineering team in Vasai can work with you to create detailed 3D models and manufacturing drawings from your concept, sketches, or equipment specs. We offer complete design-to-delivery solutions.

What's the typical lead time for custom enclosures?

Prototypes: 3–5 working days. Production batches: 2–4 weeks depending on quantity and complexity. Being based in Vasai Palghar, we offer fast local delivery to Mumbai, Thane, and surrounding areas.

Can you handle both small and large volume orders?

Yes! From single prototypes to batch production (1000+ units), our flexible manufacturing setup in Vasai handles all order sizes efficiently.
