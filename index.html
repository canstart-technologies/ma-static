About Us

We are a Vasai (East), Palghar–based precision sheet metal fabrication company delivering custom enclosures and components as job‑work. Our team supports you from RFQ to dispatch with DFM inputs, clean finishing, and reliable timelines.

  • End‑to‑end capability: punch, laser, bend, weld, powder coat, assemble
  • DFM feedback and quick prototypes to accelerate launch
  • Reliable schedules with quality checks at every stage
  • NDA‑friendly and documentation ready
