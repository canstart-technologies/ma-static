Laser Cutting Service

High‑precision laser cutting for intricate profiles, minimal burr, and repeatable accuracy across MS, SS & Aluminium sheets.

Request a Quote All Services

Overview

Our CNC laser cutting capability enables tight‑tolerance profiles, slots, ventilation patterns and aesthetic features without secondary finishing in most cases. Ideal for prototypes through production with high repeatability.

Technical Capabilities

  • Sheet sizes up to 1250 x 2500 mm
  • Thickness: MS 0.8–6 mm, SS 0.8–4 mm, Al 1–4 mm
  • Tolerances typically ±0.15 mm (profile dependent)
  • Complex patterns & micro perforations
  • Minimal burr; reduced downstream finishing
  • Nesting optimization for material efficiency

Benefits

  • High precision for assemblies and mating parts
  • Fast prototype iteration
  • Reduced tooling cost vs. stamping for low/medium volumes
  • Clean edge quality supports better coating adhesion

FAQ
